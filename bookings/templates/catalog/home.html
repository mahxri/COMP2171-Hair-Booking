Our Story

Celebrating the beauty of natural hair

Founded by Lasheka, Hair By Lasheka was born from a passion for nurturing and celebrating natural hair in the Caribbean. With expertise in protective styling, Lasheka creates looks that are as healthy as they are beautiful.

Serving clients across Jamaica and Antigua, the brand offers a streamlined, digital-first experience — making it easy to browse services, view the catalog, and book your next style without friction.
